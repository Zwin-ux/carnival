# EchoID Product Overview

EchoID is a **gamified decentralized identity platform** built on Polkadot that transforms identity management into an interactive gaming experience. The platform is themed as a "Web3 Amusement Park" where users can manage their digital identity while earning rewards, completing quests, and unlocking achievements.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Gamified Identity Management**: Users earn XP, coins, and achievements for identity-related actions
- **Blockchain-Anchored Trust**: Profiles and attestations are cryptographically verified on Substrate
- **Social Discovery**: Search and discover profiles with reputation-based filtering
- **Attestation System**: Build trust through peer-to-peer attestations and verifications

## Key Features

- **Profile Management**: Create and manage decentralized identity profiles with skills, bio, and reputation
- **Attestation System**: Give and receive attestations to build trust networks
- **Gamification**: XP progression, leveling system, achievements, daily quests, and virtual currency
- **Blockchain Integration**: Custom Substrate node with EchoID pallet for hash anchoring
- **Modern UI/UX**: Glass morphism design with carnival/amusement park theming

## Target Users

- Web3 developers and contributors building reputation
- DAO members seeking verified identity
- Job seekers in the blockchain space
- Anyone wanting to establish trust in decentralized networks

## Current Status

Post-hackathon MVP with full gamification integration, ready for production deployment and ecosystem expansion.
